About Us

History

In 2015, mr. Bautista eagerly want to help those people in the north, but he doesn't want to give them monetary right away. So he thought of a way where he can give the help they needed while teaching them how to fish. And the way of fishing is to brew some coffee.

It wasn't very hard for the team to choose the theme of the shop. Since most of the members of the family loves to take pictures, specifically a selfie and a groufie.

They also wanted to put the passion of the family be in that name. Photography and Food. That's how they came up with the name ClickEat Cafe.

It was in late 2016 where they work on that dream. The concept was put in to work. They form a team lead by their lead barista Kuya Johndel. He trained the prospect employees of the company.

January of 2017 ClickEat Cafe Bugallon opened. It was a success, that's why shortly after a few months a branch of it opened in Mangatarem. And it was in January of 2018 the third branch opened in Sual, Pangasinan.

Our Products

Tea

Not a coffee drinker? No problem because the shop happens to give what you need. A tea.

You can choose different kinds of tea-based drinks you could choose from. They offer milk tea and fruity iced tea. A delicious way of refhreshing.
